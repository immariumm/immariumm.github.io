Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pasta - 200g
  • Olive Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Tomatoes - 2 large, diced

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat olive oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix the pasta with the sauce.

Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce - 1 head
  • Olives - 10 pieces
  • Cucumber - 1, sliced
  • Feta Cheese - 100g

Instructions:

  1. Chop the lettuce and cucumber.
  2. Add olives and feta cheese.
  3. Toss the ingredients together.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.

Dessert Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate - 200g
  • Butter - 100g
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Eggs - 2

Instructions:

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
  2. Beat eggs and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Fold in the chocolate mixture.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.
